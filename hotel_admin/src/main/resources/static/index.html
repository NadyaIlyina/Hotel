Vitebsk Hotel
Our advantage
Hotel "Vitebsk", built in 1973, today is the largest hotel in our ancient city. Located in the heart of the business, cultural and historical part of Vitebsk, on the picturesque bank of the River Western Dvina. The proximity of the railway and bus stations, located near the hotel's main highways allow you to quickly reach the hotel and any other part of the city. You can take walks on foot and visit the M. Shagal Museum, the Annunciation and the Pokrovskaya Church, the Museum of Local Lore and its branches: the Art Museum, the Literary Museum, the Museum of Private Collections. A few steps from the hotel are the National Academic Drama Theater named after Yakub Kolas, the Belarusian Theater “Lyalka”, the summer amphitheater - the main venue of the International Art Festival “Slavianski Bazaar in Vitebsk” and the Directorate of the festival, the concert hall “The Center of Culture“ Vitebsk ”, a large shopping center "Vitebsk department store."
